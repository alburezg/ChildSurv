\begin{resume}

I am a social scientist specialising in inter-generational demography. 
My work has used mathematical modeling, micro-simulation techniques, and empirical analysis to explore changes in the experience of life events such as kin availability, family bereavement, and caring responsibilities across demographic regimes.
